QUEST: SCAVENGER

  • 1. Form Your Unit You must operate in a strict team of 2.
  • 2. Choose Your Roles Wisely Before deployment, your team must make a critical choice.
    • One of you will be the Solver. You will remain stationed at base command. Prepare to think critically.
    • One of you will be the Runner. You will be deployed into the field. Bring a fully charged smartphone, comfortable shoes, and prepare to move.
  • 3. Essential Gear Both operatives MUST bring 2-way communication devices (AirPods, Bluetooth headphones, or wired earphones with a built-in microphone). The Runner may carry a powerbank (optional).
  • 4. Trust No One But Your Partner Communication is your only lifeline. If you cannot communicate effectively under pressure, your mission will fail.
  • 5. Speed is Essential You will be tracked. You will be timed. Faster execution yields higher rewards.
Do not ask Command for details. Await further instructions.
